Updates

We are excited to announce the release of DaydreamX v2, a major update that brings a host of new features, performance improvements, and a sleek new user interface. This version is designed to enhance your experience and provide more customization options.

  • Performance Enhancements: Faster load times and smoother transitions for a seamless experience.
  • New Customization Options: More themes, layouts, and settings.
  • Improved Security: Enhanced proxy features and backend.
  • Bug Fixes: Addressed and fixed various issues and improved overall stability.
