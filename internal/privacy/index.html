Code Obfuscation & Build Process

To protect our intellectual property and infrastructure, DayDreamX uses code and font obfuscation during the production build process. This means:

  • JavaScript code is minified and obfuscated to make it harder to reverse engineer
  • Font files may be obfuscated to prevent unauthorized extraction
  • This does not affect functionality or your privacy in any way
  • The obfuscation is primarily to protect Night+ systems and prevent unauthorized modifications

Full, unobfuscated source code is always available in our GitLab repository. The obfuscation only applies to production builds.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Stored Locally

DayDreamX stores the following information locally in your browser using LocalStorage and IndexedDB:

  • Settings & Preferences: Theme choices, proxy settings, search engine preferences, tab cloaking configurations
  • Browsing Data: Bookmarks, browsing history, tab states, and shortcuts
  • Extensions Data: User-installed extensions and their configurations
  • Authentication Tokens: Night+ login tokens if you use our optional account system

Important: This data is stored ONLY on your device. We do not have access to this information unless you explicitly sync it through Night+.

1.2 Server Logs

When you use DayDreamX, our servers may temporarily log:

  • IP addresses (anonymized and not stored long-term)
  • Browser type and version
  • Access times and dates
  • Error messages and performance metrics

These logs are used solely for maintaining service stability, debugging, and security purposes. They are automatically deleted after a short retention period.

1.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • We do NOT track the websites you visit through our proxy
  • We do NOT store or analyze your browsing history on our servers
  • We do NOT sell, rent, or share your data with third parties
  • We do NOT use tracking cookies or analytics scripts
  • We do NOT monitor the content you access through the service

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the minimal information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the DayDreamX service
  • Improve and optimize service performance
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • Protect against abuse and malicious activity
  • Respond to user support requests
  • Communicate service updates and important notices

We will never use your information for advertising purposes or share it with third-party advertisers.

3. Night+ Premium Service

Night+ is our optional premium subscription service that provides enhanced features beyond the core DayDreamX functionality. Using Night+ is completely optional, and all core proxy features remain available without a subscription.

3.1 Information Collected with Night+ Account

If you choose to create a Night+ account, we collect and store the following:

  • Account Information: Username, email address (optional), encrypted password
  • Subscription Data: Payment method (processed by third-party payment processors), subscription tier, billing dates
  • Authentication Tokens: Secure tokens to maintain your login session
  • Account Activity: Login timestamps, last active date, subscription status changes

3.2 Night+ Sync Data

If you enable Night+ Cloud Sync, the following data is encrypted and stored on our servers:

  • Settings & Preferences: Theme choices, proxy settings, customization options
  • Profiles: Your browser profiles (unlimited for Night+ users, up to 3 for free users)
  • Bookmarks: Your saved bookmarks and bookmark folders
  • History: Browsing history (only if sync is enabled for history)
  • Extensions: List of installed extensions and their settings
  • Custom Themes: Your custom theme configurations and backgrounds
  • Server Preferences: Selected WISP servers and proxy configurations (premium server URLs remain concealed)

All sync data is encrypted before transmission and storage. We cannot access the actual content of your synced data.

You can disable sync at any time and delete all synced data from our servers through your Night+ account settings.

3.2.1 Premium Server Infrastructure

Night+ subscribers gain access to premium WISP servers and proxy backends that provide enhanced performance, reliability, and speed. These premium servers are:

  • Dynamically provided: Server URLs are fetched from secure endpoints only accessible with valid Night+ authentication
  • Concealed: Premium server URLs are not publicly disclosed or visible in client-side code to prevent abuse
  • Monitored: We may log connection attempts to premium servers for security and abuse prevention
  • Exclusive: Only available to active Night+ subscribers with valid authentication

Premium server access is immediately revoked when your Night+ subscription ends or is cancelled. We do not store or expose premium server URLs to unauthorized users.

Connection logs to premium servers may include: timestamp, authentication status, and connection success/failure. We do not log the websites you visit through these proxies.

3.3 Payment Processing

Night+ subscription payments are processed by third-party payment processors. We do not store your full credit card numbers or payment details on our servers. Payment processors handle all sensitive financial information in compliance with PCI-DSS standards.

We only receive confirmation of successful payments and basic subscription status from our payment processors.

3.4 Usage Analytics (Night+ Only)

Night+ subscribers who opt-in may receive privacy-respecting usage analytics, such as:

  • Total browsing sessions
  • Feature usage statistics (which features you use most)
  • Performance metrics (page load times, connection quality)
  • Device and browser information

These analytics are aggregated and anonymized. We never track specific websites you visit or the content you access. Analytics can be disabled in your Night+ settings.

3.5 Night+ Account Deletion

You can delete your Night+ account at any time. Upon deletion:

  • All account information is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • All synced data is permanently deleted from our servers
  • Your subscription is immediately cancelled (no refunds for partial periods)
  • Access to premium features is immediately revoked
  • Core DayDreamX features remain available without Night+

We may retain limited billing records for tax and legal compliance purposes as required by law.

Night+ is completely optional. You can use DayDreamX fully without creating an account. All proxy features and core functionality work without Night+.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

  • All connections to DayDreamX are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Night+ authentication uses secure, industry-standard encryption
  • We implement security best practices in our codebase
  • Access to server infrastructure is restricted and monitored
  • We conduct regular security audits and updates

WISP Protocol Encryption

DayDreamX uses the WISP (WebSocket Interface for Secure Proxying) protocol developed by MercuryWorkshop to encrypt and transmit your browsing data. This ensures that your proxied traffic is encrypted end-to-end between your browser and our proxy servers.

Learn more about WISP: WISP Protocol Specification

Traffic Filtering on Production Servers

On our production servers, network traffic is filtered using Cloudflare Family DNS for basic content filtering and security. However:

  • We cannot read your encrypted traffic. Due to WISP encryption, your browsing content remains private
  • Cloudflare DNS filtering occurs at the network level and only blocks known malicious or inappropriate domains
  • This filtering helps protect our infrastructure and users from malware and harmful content
  • Your specific browsing destinations and page contents are not visible to us or Cloudflare through the proxy

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
